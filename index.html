    John J Kauflin

    Welcome to my website, and yes, it's mostly about me. However, I hope this site also reflects the experiences I've had with all the wonderful friends, family, bandmates, and workmates I've had in my life. It's been my pleasure to know you all. This website is a place where I can collect and present pictures, videos, and music to honor the memory of people and things. I hope it does justice to all of the people and things I have loved.

    My Families

    I've been involved with some great and interesting families including my Mom's, my Dad's, my Wife's, and my own. I appreciate my Mom taking all of those snapshots over the years, and my Dad for shooting the 8mm home movies when I was growing up. They are priceless memories of a great childhood.

    My Bands

    Music has been one of the great joys of my life and I've had the honor and privilege of playing with some great musicians. I've been in bands with a group of friends since High School and we have created a music community that continues to this day.
